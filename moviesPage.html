Movies On Demand Right Now :)

1. The Avengers movies series is a great way to enjoy the movie night with friends and family root for fan favorite superheros and enjoy a golden series of throwback movies. Packed with the right amounts of action, drama, and comedy, it makes watching every moment a wild ride you won't regret.

All the Marvel Films (Avengers included)

2. Uncorked is a recent movie released on Netflix about a young man who decides to pursue his own entrepuinal path instead of joining the family business. Full of comedy as well as the right dose of drama this movie will not fail to disappoint the family for a good movie night.

Uncorked Review

3. To All the Boys I have Ever loved is a teen-oriented romantic comedy about a girl who decides to write love letters to all her previous crushes. However, she finds out to her surprise that a mysterious figure mailed out her letters, which sets into motion a funny and dramatic sequence of events.

To All the Boys I've Ever Loved Review

4. The Star Wars movie series is considered among the very best of movies ever produced. It is an instant classic that is extremely popular at movie marathons and even has a cult following till this day. It has all the elements of a all-time great movie and doesn't fail to deliver on the rush you will get as you travel throughout the galaxy far, far, away.

Star Wars overview

Binge-Worthy TV shows

TV-14 Recommendations

1. The Umbrella Academy is a take on the typical superhero TV shows where a group of orphans are adopted by a reclusive billionare who alledgely prepares them to save the world, until one day when he dies, and the children are forced to find out what happened.

The Umbrella Academy Reviews

2. The Good Place is a comedy that revolves around a woman who is surprised to have made it into heaven. However, her daily adventures with the pure and moral people around her make her realize that sometimes it isn't too late too change.

The Good Place Reviews

3. Lucifer is a interesting take on the typical God/Devil TV shows as it portrays the Devil as a handsome young man who has moved to LA and works as a consultant for the LAPD with Detective Chloe Decker, with all the fun you would expect the Devil to have.

Lucifer Reviews

MA-rated Recommendations

1. Narcos is an action show that is based off the rise of the drug trade and cartels in Columbia and later Mexico. Despite being gritty, violent, and not for the faint of heart, it has earned critical acclaim for its pace, acting, plot, and how realistic it is.

Narcos Reviews

2. Dave Chappelle is a series of stand-up comedy shows starring award winning comedian Dave Chappelle. He never fails to make his audience laugh with his witty jokes about various aspects of society who isn't afraid to let loose.

Dave Chappelle full list

3. Tiger King is the latest hit documentary on Joe Exotic, a larger than life man who owned a zoo full of exotic cats. The documentary does a phenomenal job covering his wild and erratic personality and his legal battles with Carol Baskin, an animal activist commited to shutting down his zoo.

Tiger King overview

4. The Blacklist is the classic crime show that revolves around a criminal mastermind named Raymond Reddington, who joins forces with Special FBI Agent Elizabeth Keen to catch some of the world's most elusive criminals. It doesn't fail to disappoint, while also leaving viewers yearning for more and the mysterious past of Keen and Reddington.

Blacklist review
